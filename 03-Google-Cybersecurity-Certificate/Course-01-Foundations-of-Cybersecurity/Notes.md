> Module 1 - Foundations of Cybersecurity

## What I Learned

- Cybersecurity is about protecting people, systems, networks, and data.
- The CIA Triad (Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability) is the foundation of information security.
- A Security Analyst is responsible for monitoring, detecting, investigating, and responding to security incidents.
- Protecting Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is a key responsibility of every organization.
- Technical skills like Python, SQL, SIEM, and IDS are essential, but communication and problem-solving are equally important.
- Continuous learning is necessary because cybersecurity threats constantly evolve.
